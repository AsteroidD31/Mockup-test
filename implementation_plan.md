
Project Overview

Build a premium, high-converting pizzeria landing page inspired by luxury product websites and modern restaurant brands.

The design should focus on a cinematic experience where the pizza remains the primary visual element throughout the journey.

The entire website should maintain a dark theme with black backgrounds, allowing the food photography and subtle motion effects to stand out.

Hero Animations

Page Load Animation

Pizza:

* Scale from 0.7 to 1
* Rotate from -15° to 0°
* Fade in

* Slide upward
* Fade in sequentially

CTA:

Duration:

⸻

Floating Animation

After load:

Pizza should continuously:

* Float vertically
* Move slowly
* Rotate subtly

Motion should feel natural and premium.

Avoid exaggerated movement.

⸻

Mouse Interaction

As cursor moves:

Pizza should slightly tilt.

Movement range:

* Maximum 5°

Create depth without distraction.

⸻

Scroll Animation

When user scrolls:

Pizza:

* Scales up
* Rotates slowly
* Moves upward

This transition should lead naturally into the About section.
